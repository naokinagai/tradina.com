Our Story

Established in 2007, TRADINA Corporation began as a pioneering inbound travel agency when Japan was still an emerging destination for international travelers. We provided some of the earliest comprehensive travel, dining, and hotel information services in English.

Today, we have evolved into a specialized professional translation company with expertise in corporate document translation.

Based in Toranomon, Tokyo, we operate as a global remote organization, connecting skilled translators worldwide.
